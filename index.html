About Us

Welcome to Kian & Ite's Pizza Van! We are passionate about serving delicious, freshly made pizzas at New Bridge Academy.

Our food truck is dedicated to bringing you the best flavors and quality ingredients.

Our team is committed to providing a friendly and enjoyable experience for all our customers.

Whether you're a student, staff member, or visitor, we invite you to stop by and enjoy a slice of our mouthwatering pizzas.
